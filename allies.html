◈ About
An organisation that investigates interplanar disturbances and hunts monsters of myth-like repute. They are not particularly well-known to commonfolk, but appear to have friends in high places.

◈ Leoneiros Caimys

Class Monk, Path of Tranquility
Race Sea elf, Ephemera
Attitude Friendly
Location Rellholdt City
Archetype Brawler. Melee combat, healing, utility magic.

A pirate and part-time monster hunter who used to sail with the Red Islanders. While one of the more senior members of the organisation, he has always preferred more straightforward hunting assignments over investigative types.
◈ Dursanna Illadys

Class Druid, Circle of Stars
Race Eladrin elf
Attitude Amiable
Location Faycrest
Archetype Shapeshifter. Wild shape, healing, nature magic.

A quiet individual who has, in recent years, taken over the position of resident librarian.
◈ Ramel Knight

Class Cleric, Death Domain
Race Tiefling, Ephemera (Pelor)
Attitude Allied
Location Aberast
Archetype Necromancer. Healing, war magic, ritual magic.

A necromancer and resident healing specialist, as well as liaison to the Soramyre forest. He seems to have more history than most with the Cult, and has allegedly successfully performed the Ephemera ritual.
◈ Ninos Knight

Class Bard, College of Swords ; Warlock, Fiend pact
Race Tiefling, Ephemera (Selune)
Attitude Pleasant
Location Aberast
Notable Skills Spellsword. War magic, melee combat, healing.

A hunter often accompanied by a white hellhound called Miro. He is typically assigned as secondary support for the various field teams.
